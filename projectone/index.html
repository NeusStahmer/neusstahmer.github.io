MAGAZINE RE-DESIGN
for WeeklyDatingInsider

What is Weekly Dating Insider?

Weekly Dating Insider is a magazine and comparison site for dating websites. The product has already been live since 2016, being active in different countries.

About the project:

The goal of this project was to redesign the product and give it a magazine feeling. The amount of articles that the site offers increased and we wanted to give them more importance. The redesign would include a new color palette and iconography.
