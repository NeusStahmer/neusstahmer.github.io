What is Space Travel?

Space Travel is a travel agency that wants to fly their customers to Mars.

About the project:

Space Travel is a project we did for the Master in Digital Experiences, I completed in BAU Design College of Barcelona. The goal was the creation of wireframes, chose the Typography and Colour Palette, have a specific Typography scale for each device. Last but not least, the pictures were selected, that would give the whole project a visual impact.
