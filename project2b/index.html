Email Marketing

Email Marketing can be challenging, when trying to find the right balance between not being to invase and deliver a solution to a problem our user may have. This is why a good design concept is so important, since it will make the difference between good news and another email filling the inbox.

I started doing my first Email concepts in my role as a Marketing Manager, to run them on my own campaigns.

Slowly I started to be interested in how these templates are build and started to play around with the code. This was my first contact with HTML and CSS.

On the other hand, we want to make sure that we're fitting our customers needs, showing them how we can be a solution to what they're looking for, not being overwhelming with unnecessary content.

Later on, after I switched my career path, going in to the design direction, I took my little knowledge in email coding with me and developed it further.
