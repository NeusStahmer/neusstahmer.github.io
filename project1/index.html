Weekly Dating Insider

Re-Design

What is Weekly Dating Insider?

Weekly Dating Insider is a magazine and comparison site for dating websites. The product has already been live since 2016, being active in different countries.

The Goal

The goal of this project was to redesign the product and give it a magazine feeling. The amount of articles that the site offers increased and we wanted to give them more importance. The redesign would include a new color palette and layout.

My Role

Visual Designer in charge of the re-design.

The Quiz

Later on, we added a quiz, that would help the users to pick the perfect site for them.
